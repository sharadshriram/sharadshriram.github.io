MyCI: A campus chatbot powered by human-computation

Robot Assistant for single mothers with limited cognitive capabilities

Using the NAO robot as the assistant to assist congitively challenged, single mothers in preparing breakfast. The objective is to give feedback and suggestion on the selected item for breakfast based on whether the item is healthy for the child or not. The experiments were done as a Wizard of Oz experiment to control the robot's interaction with the mothers.
